How Points Work 🎮

Every message you send can earn points. Here's how:

📝 Base Points

Harder activities are worth more points from the start:

  • Beginner — 8 pts
  • Intermediate — 14 pts
  • Advanced — 22 pts
  • Story Mode — 30 pts

🔥 Streak Bonus

Write 3+ correct sentences in a row without a mistake, and you'll earn a growing bonus. One mistake resets the streak — so accuracy matters!

✅ Self-Correction Bonus

If Lexi points out a mistake and you get it right on your next try, you get bonus points for actually learning from the feedback.

📚 Vocabulary Bonus

Using new words you haven't used yet this session earns a small bonus. Mix it up!

🏆 Ranks

Your total points (plus trying harder activities) level you up through ranks:

Bronze Silver Gold Platinum Diamond Crown Ace Conqueror

Gold and above need you to try harder activities, not just Beginner ones. Conqueror is the rarest rank — you need a huge score, Story Mode experience, AND a 10-message clean streak. Very few reach it!
